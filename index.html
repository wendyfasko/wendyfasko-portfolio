MoXy – Clean Tech & Urban Sustainability

MoXy is an innovative clean tech startup developing modular moss bioreactors that transform urban data center emissions into clean air. Our bioreactors absorb over 10 kg of CO₂ per square meter annually by reusing low-grade waste heat from data centers, boosting moss photosynthesis efficiency.

Equipped with IoT sensors, these walls provide real-time ESG monitoring, turning environmental impact into measurable, investable assets. This tech addresses an intersection of multi-billion-dollar markets including data center cooling, urban air purification, and modular green walls.

As B2B Project Manager & ESG Technical Writer, I lead deployment logistics, investor communications, and technical documentation, bridging the gap between engineering teams and stakeholders to ensure smooth project delivery and impactful storytelling.

We are currently raising €500,000 pre-seed funding to deploy the first full-scale prototype on a rooftop data center in Torino, aiming to scale urban clean air innovation across Europe.
